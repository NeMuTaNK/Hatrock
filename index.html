Meet the Sponsor!

David Pierre-Louis, the CEO of Haiti Coffee, is looking toward feeding profits back into Haiti to educate farmers and entrepreneurs helping them grow their potential markets, increase their income and improve production.

Our mission...

We are creating a digital platform that connects the entire ecosystem, including farmers, buyers, roasters, consumers, and investors who are interested in supporting the recovery of Haiti’s coffee industry and bringing this unique product to the global market.
