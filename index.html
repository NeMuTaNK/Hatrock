Our Sponsor

David Pierre-Louis, the CEO of Haiti Coffee, i created Haiti Coffee Co. with the goal of reviving the Haitian economy, and to help farmers and entrepreneurs grow their potential markets, increase their incomes, and improve production.

Our Goal

We began working with Haiti Coffee Co. early January and over the past 5 months, our product and vision for this project has gone through many iterations and changed shape and structure numerous times. The goals of this project have remained to 1) enhance the visibility of the Haitian Coffee industry with farm-to-cup transparency and 2) create a digital platform that connects the entire Haitian coffee ecosystem, from farmers to roasters to consumers. We are executing these goals through building a custom Shopify theme that serves as an online community to connect every stakeholder in the Haitian Coffee Industry. This theme is customizable, so after completion of this project Haiti Coffee will be able to update and style their website with very minimal programming.

Our Users



As you can see, Haiti Coffee Co. has an incredibly wide reach. Our goal with step of the process, through each development cycle, is to ensure that our technologies are compatible and understandable to our users across the globe. This is in part to increase education and awareness abou the Haitian economy after devasting natural disasters in the early 2000's, but also to increase accessibility to a global market.

Our Process

In the beginning of our project, we pitched several ideas for the redevelopment of Haiti Coffee Co.'s front-end interface. We wanted to make sure that we were delivering a high-quality product using skills that we had honed over the past few years. However, we quickly realized that from an operational standpoint, this wouldn't be feasible for hand-off after we finish Capstone. We then pivoted back to the platform that HCC was hosting their e-commerce shop on- Shopify.

Initially, Shopify was really complex to unravel, but videos like these and an excellent mentor who specializes in theme development for Shopify helped get us rolling.
